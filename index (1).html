About Our Association

Our Mission

To foster community engagement, promote social welfare, and create opportunities for personal and collective growth among our members.

Our Vision

A thriving community where every member contributes to and benefits from our collective strength and shared resources.

Our Values

  • Integrity and Transparency
  • Community Service
  • Mutual Support
  • Cultural Preservation
  • Youth Development

Constitution & Bylaws

Association Constitution

Article I - Name and Purpose

The name of this organization shall be the Community Association, organized for the purpose of promoting community welfare and member development.

Article II - Membership

Membership is open to all individuals who share our values and commit to our community standards.

Article III - Governance

The Association shall be governed by an elected Executive Committee, accountable to all members.
